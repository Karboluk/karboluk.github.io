Isthariel was a powerful Potentate in the Cimmerian Empire, using her magical prowess to dominate a large section of the city and suppress any potential challengers for almost 200 years. During this time, she managed to defeat her only equal, Quidun, by using guile to waylay him long enough to create an Obelisk that channeled large amounts of power to herself (by tapping what is now the active volcano at Uxtatli), allowing her great resilience, regenerative power, and magical energy.

Isthariel was eventually exiled by a cabal of lesser wizards and divine practitioners, forcing her to flee from her own palace onto the plane of Limbo, where she spent the timeless days preparing; it is there that she encountered agents of an Elder God, Orryx, who told her the story of the rise of Thalassus and the fall of the ancient culture of Bassaria.

This information taught Isthariel that there was a way to gain even greater power and get revenge on her foes. In pursuing this goal over the sleepless timescape of the plane, the merciless Isthariel lost any remaining vestige of kindness or restraint under the sanity-draining tutelage of Orryx’s acolytes. Shortly after this she ascended fully and returned to the world as the God Zatar.

Her wrath was the cause of the fall of the Cimmerian Empire, as she systematically destroyed all those who opposed her, and anyone around them. Her final act during her Ascent to Godhood was to cast the City of Cimmeria down the ground from the heights, burying it into the landscape and creating a large bay North of what is now Port Rain, and vertically approximately above Applewood.

Several lesser gods, such as Jaraxx, the Rot, attempted to intervene, so once Cimmeria was in ruins, Zatar turned her eyes to the gods as the new source of her unbridled, raging, insanity.
