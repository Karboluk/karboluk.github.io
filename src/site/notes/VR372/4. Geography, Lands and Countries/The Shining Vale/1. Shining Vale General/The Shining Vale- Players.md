"The Shining Vale" refers to a wide valley running in a roughly East-West axis, some 700 miles southeast and inland of the site of fallen [[Cimmeria\|Cimmeria]]. It is so named due to the high volume and quality of gems found in the Barrier Range of mountains which forms most of the natural southern border of the Vale. 

</div></div>



The Vale is divided into 4 Duchies, [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/The Shining Vale/2. Duchy Revanne/Duchy Revanne-Players\|Duchy Revanne]], [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/The Shining Vale/5. Duchy Corwyn/Duchy Corwyn-Players\|Duchy Corwyn]], [[Duchy Truscia-Players\|Duchy Truscia]], and [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/The Shining Vale/4. Duchy Redhill/Duchy Redhill-Players\|Duchy Redhill]]. Each is ruled by a Duke, who in turn owe allegiance to the King in [[Haut-Revanne-Players\|Haut-Revanne]].
