Duchy Redhill was founded by [[Ephraim Redhill- Players\|Ephraim Redhill]] at the site of [[Ironhelm- Players\|Ironhelm]] in VR 32, after an exhaustive search for the best location to begin a proper Dwarven delve. Finding iron, precious metals and gem veins in plentiful supply, the mining continues to this day. Many of the work camps and temporary settlements in the hills leading up to the Barrier Range, which were founded in the search for Ironhelm, have now become vital towns in their own right, for the purpose of trading, quarrying and lumber harvesting.

The Capital of Duchy Redhill is [[Ironhelm- Players\|Ironhelm]], and the current Duke is Rugan Redhill, son of [[Ephraim Redhill- Players\|Ephraim Redhill]]. He has been the Duke since VR 154, when Ephraim officially abdicated in Rugan's favor in order to explore and found lands to the east, which became [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/The Shining Vale/5. Duchy Corwyn/Duchy Corwyn-Players\|Duchy Corwyn]] only a few years later in VR 156.
