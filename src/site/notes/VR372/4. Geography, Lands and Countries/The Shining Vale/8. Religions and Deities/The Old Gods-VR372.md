The so-called Old Gods, other than [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/The Fates/The Fates-VR372\|The Fates]], are generally not worshiped in the Vale. The educated among the populace are aware of them, however. Some here and there may pay them some heed directly.
