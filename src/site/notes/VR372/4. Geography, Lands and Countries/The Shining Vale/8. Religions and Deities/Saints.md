[[ZZZ. Private DM Info/6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Volkanus/Lady Racca, the Planner\|St. Aeonath]]
	Patron of Civilization and the Monarchy. Aeonath was the first of the Revannian Kings, and the leader of the adventurers who cleared the Vale for settlement. Aeonath's symbol is a Silver griffon rampant on a white field, or simply a griffon rampant. Aeonath reigned until his death in VR 42, and was sanctified shortly afterwards. The Cathedral to St. Aeonath is in [[Haut-Revanne-Players\|Haut-Revanne]].

[[ZZZ. Private DM Info/6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Maahes/Kor, the Tireless\|St. Kor]]
	Patron of Strength and endurance. A member of Aeonath's group, Kor was a stalwart warrior renowned for his preference to fight without armor, and the effectiveness with which he used his great axe. Kor went off to great adventure after the Vale was settled, but his axe now hangs in the Cathedral to St. Kor in [[Corwyn City- Players\|Corwyn City]]. Kor was sanctified in absentia in VR 20, the first of the Saints.
