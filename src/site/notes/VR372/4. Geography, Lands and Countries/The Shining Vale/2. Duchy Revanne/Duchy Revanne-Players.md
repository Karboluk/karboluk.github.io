Duchy Revanne was founded in VR -56, starting from [[Bas-Revanne- Players\|Bas-Revanne]] in the northwest and moving east and south. [[Haut-Revanne-Players\|Haut-Revanne]] was founded in VR -16, and was decided to be the capital at the coronation of [[Aeonath- Players\|Aeonath]] which marks the beginning of VR 1. 

The Capital of Duchy Revanne is [[Bas-Revanne- Players\|Bas-Revanne]].
