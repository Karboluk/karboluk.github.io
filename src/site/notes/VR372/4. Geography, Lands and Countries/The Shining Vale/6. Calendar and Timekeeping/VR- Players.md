Vale Reckoning (VR) is the calendar system used in the Shining Vale during the time of the Revannian Monarchy.  VR 1 began at the coronation of King Aeonath the Civilizer, leader of the heroes who pacified the Vale, in the settlement of Haut-Revanne.
