The Luxan Empire existed in the distant past in the place now partially occupied by [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale General/The Shining Vale-VR372\|The Shining Vale]], [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/2. Others/2. Subdurni/Subdurni-VR372\|Subdurni]], [[Lorica-VR372\|Lorica]], and [[The High Depths-VR372\|The High Depths]], having fallen long before the elves of [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale/2. Duchy Revanne/The Enclave-VR372\|The Enclave]] or the [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/2. Others/Sea Kingdom of Kilar-VR372\|Sea Kingdom of Kilar]] arrived. Their sites are clearly constructed on intersections of lines of power. From the archeological evidence, they appear to have been mostly Tabaxi, with with a sizable slave-caste of dwarves.

It seems clear that the Luxans worshiped [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Helios the Radiant Truth-VR372\|Helios]] primarily, and evidence of worship of later gods is absent from their temples- most notably [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Volkanus the Maker and Reshaper of all Things-VR372\|Volkanus]], or more accurately Moradin, as dwarven construction would normally carry. 

This notable absence asks a much more interesting question, which seems to have two equally interesting possible answers: Either this indicates the presence of Dwarves prior to [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Volkanus the Maker and Reshaper of all Things-VR372\|Volkanus]]'s emergence in [[Thracia-VR372\|Thracia]], which is problematic as he is commonly regarded as the Dwarven creator god, or somehow the normal dwarven cultural tendencies were erased or suppressed during this time; not an easy feat.
