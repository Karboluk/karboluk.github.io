The [[City of Cimmeria-VR372\|flying city of Cimmeria]] fell after the ascension of [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Zatar the Ravaging Storm-VR372\|Zatar]], when she wrought her revenge upon the council of wizards that had driven her away. With a mighty blow, the city she had sought to rule for so long became as a meteor down to the land. Much was destroyed along the coast where the impact happened, including the [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/2. Others/Sea Kingdom of Kilar-VR372\|Sea Kingdom of Kilar]] and the Cimmerian Army who had recently occupied it (having found it vacated).

Those in the far-flung colonies of Cimmeria were unaffected physically by the event, but the vacuum of power at the top has caused political upheaval and war since then. Nearby colonies such as [[Lorica-VR372\|Lorica]] were more troubled, as their ongoing conquests now had no support.

Of those in the City itself, a very small fraction survived. These refugees went on to found [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale General/The Shining Vale-VR372\|The Shining Vale]].
