Quidun the Wise and Beneficent was the lich ruler of the [[City of Cimmeria-VR372\|City of Cimmeria]], and the [[Cimmerian Empire-VR372\|Cimmerian Empire]], for over a millennia. Seeking to [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood-VR372\|ascend]] to Godhood, Quidun was systematically developing a powerful network from which to draw the requisite energy- from people, from natural features, and from pre-existing arcane and divine power structures. 

Nearing the culmination of his efforts, and likely because of the proximity of that event, his great rival [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/2. Others/4. Cimmeria/Isthariel the Channeler-VR372\|Isthariel]] overcame him and deposed him. 

Now reduced in form, Quidun seeks to build power and influence once more.
