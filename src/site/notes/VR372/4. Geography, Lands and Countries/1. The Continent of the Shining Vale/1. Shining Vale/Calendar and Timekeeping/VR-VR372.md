Vale Reckoning (VR) is the calendar system used in the Shining Vale during the time of the Revannian Monarchy.  VR 1 began at the coronation of [[Aeonath-VR372\|King Aeonath the Civilizer]], leader of the heroes who pacified the Vale, in the settlement of [[Haut-Revanne-VR372\|Haut-Revanne]].
