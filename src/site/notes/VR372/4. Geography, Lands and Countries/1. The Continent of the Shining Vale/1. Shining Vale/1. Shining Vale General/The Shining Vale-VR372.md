"The Shining Vale" refers to a wide valley running in a roughly East-West axis, some 700 miles southeast and inland of the site of fallen [[Cimmeria\|Cimmeria]]. It is so named due to the high volume and quality of gems found in the Barrier Range of mountains which forms most of the natural southern border of the Vale. 

The Vale is divided into 4 Duchies, [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale/2. Duchy Revanne/Duchy Revanne-VR372\|Duchy Revanne]], [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale/5. Duchy Corwyn/Duchy Corwyn-VR372\|Duchy Corwyn]], [[Duchy Truscia-VR372\|Duchy Truscia]], and [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale/4. Duchy Redhill/Duchy Redhill-VR372\|Duchy Redhill]]. Each is ruled by a Duke, who in turn owe allegiance to the King in [[Haut-Revanne-VR372\|Haut-Revanne]].
