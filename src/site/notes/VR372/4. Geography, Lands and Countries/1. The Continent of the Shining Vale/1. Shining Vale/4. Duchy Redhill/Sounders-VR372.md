Sounders are a special order of dwarven miners, who use advanced sonic techniques to probe the integrity, and sometimes content, of the stone walls ahead of them. They will likely never know this, but this technique was gifted to them by [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Thalassus of the Oceans-VR372\|Thalassus]] and is nearly indistinguishable from whalesong.
