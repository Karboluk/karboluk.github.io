Duchy Corwyn was founded at [[Corwyn City-VR372\|Corwyn City]] by Duke [[Ephraim Redhill-VR372\|Ephraim Redhill]] in VR 156, having successfully established [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale/4. Duchy Redhill/Duchy Redhill-VR372\|Duchy Redhill]], settling his people and ensuring prosperity for the Vale with their mining, quarrying and lumber industries.  Duke Redhill wished to memorialize his fallen brother, [[Corwyn Redhill-VR372\|Corwyn]], by writing his name across the land.

The capital of Duchy Corwyn is [[Corwyn City-VR372\|Corwyn City]], and the current Duke is Glinda Redhill, daughter of [[Ephraim Redhill-VR372\|Ephraim Redhill]]. She has been the Duke since VR 277 when Ephraim passed away.
