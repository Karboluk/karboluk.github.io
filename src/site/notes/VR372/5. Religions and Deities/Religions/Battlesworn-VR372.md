In [[Thracia-VR372\|Thracia]] the Battlesworn believe that if war was ever to cease, Maahes (or Polemos, as they know him) would die; and when war broke out again a new God of War would emerge, causing massive destructions due to their Ascension and then most certainly being less interested in honor than blood-thirst.  

It is for this reason that they encourage constant war and competition between the Kingdoms, and their doctrine and traditional position of power have been persuasive enough to gain acceptance from the people and tolerance from the other faiths.
