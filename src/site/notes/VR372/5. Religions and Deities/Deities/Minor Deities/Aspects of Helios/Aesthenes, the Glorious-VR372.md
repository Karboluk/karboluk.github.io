Aesthenes is a [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods-VR372\|Minor God]].
###### Regional Names: 
St. Agatha ([[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale/The Shining Vale-VR372\|The Shining Vale]], [[Lausanne-VR372\|Lausanne]]), She Who Inspires
###### Domains: 
Life, Light, Peace, Trickery

###### Regional Worship: 

It is not uncommon for a temple to Aesthenes to bless and sell clothing and beauty products which are enchanted to provide an illusory appearance for the clothing and/or the wearer.



## Nature:

Generally depicted as a partially nude, picturesque woman, mortal artists trying to portray her always complain that they haven't got it quite right; they are correct, but they may not have realized that success in this effort is not possible.

Aesthenes is the Muse of Beauty, and works to make all things beautiful. Her standard approach is to create and improve, only advocating destruction in the worst case scenarios. She works on people, lands, buildings, creatures- nothing is beyond her gaze.

## Origins:

During the reconstruction of [[Bassaria-VR372\|Bassaria]] after the [[Sundering of the Lands-VR372\|Sundering of the Lands]], the once-majestic civilization was in ruin. Cities had been shaken to rubble, and sometimes even split by the newly-formed oceans; and despite the new rule of justice under [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Helios/Themis, the Righteous-VR372\|Themis]], the people still lost hope. [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Helios the Radiant Truth-VR372\|Helios]] saw their despair and resolved to give them reason for hope and cheer; and thus in an instant was born Aesthenes, the very idea of Beauty. 


## Activity:

Aesthenes is one of the only minor gods that does not generally appear in her physical form to mortals, instead opting to visit them in dreams, visions and in reverie. The few times she does visit physically is when she needs to command rather than her usual choice to influence, to enthrall, to compel, or to seduce.
