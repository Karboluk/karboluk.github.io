Aesclepius is a [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods-VR372\|Minor God]].
###### Regional Names: 
St Lucia ([[Lausanne-VR372\|Lausanne]], [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale/1. Shining Vale General/The Shining Vale-VR372\|The Shining Vale]]), He Who Restores
###### Domains: 
Forge, Life

###### Regional Worship: 

Temples to Aesclepius are rare, but a shrine or icon is present in many temples to other gods, as a show of respect to the original healer. Some workshops will do the same, for the mending of broken things is not so different. 

## Nature:

Aesclepius is often depicted as a patrician scholar, a learned man who understands the workings of the body and the soul, but in truth this depiction stems from the prejudices of mortal society more than of Aesclepius' true form. If a mortal ever saw the actual form of Aesclepius, a turquoise-scaled snake with human arms and legs, I'm not sure healing would be the thought in their mind. 

Aesclepius is literally the font of healing magic in the world, which means that any healing from any god, enchantment or potion, flows from him. He fuels this by inhabiting the very center of [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Helios the Radiant Truth-VR372\|Helios]]' being, staying in the sun as it turns about the world.

## Origins:


In the aftermath of the [[Sundering of the Lands-VR372\|Sundering of the Lands]], in the reconstruction of [[Bassaria-VR372\|Bassaria]] after [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Helios/Themis, the Righteous-VR372\|Themis]], [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Helios/Aesthenes, the Glorious-VR372\|Aesthenes]], and [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Helios/Parvati, the Accepting-VR372\|Parvati]] has come into being and done their work, [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Helios the Radiant Truth-VR372\|Helios]] was satisfied that mortals would no longer actively destroy themselves; at least, in the short term. Then, as is his way, he saw a way to improve the situation even more: what if they could not only not kill, but what if they could repair? Thus was born Aesclepius, the very idea of Healing.

## Activity:

The constant draw on Aesclepius for healing has progressed to the point that he can not leave his position inside [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Helios the Radiant Truth-VR372\|Helios]], but he does have agents and followers in the world with whom he regularly communes. He uses them to promote the advancement of medicines and healing magic, and to ensure that needs in this regards are being met. 

*And allow me to assure you, agents of the God of Healing are nearly impossible to kill. My ratio for success is below 10 percent. 
-Reyvan*
