Frouros is a [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods-VR372\|Minor God]].

###### Regional Names: 
Colossus ([[Thracia-VR372\|Thracia]]), Vigilance, He Who Watches So We May Rest
###### Domains: 
Forge, Order, Peace, War

###### Regional Worship: 
Other than in [[Thracia-VR372\|Thracia]], explicit worship of Frouros has gone out of fashion- with most large nations being more interested in warfare instead of defensive oversight, when it comes to battle. Occasionally a city state, castle, or other target will start to worship him though, so he is not entirely relegated to the background or to common sayings, like some gods have been.

## Nature:

Frouros stands as a sentinel between the mortals of this world and the creatures that want to invade. He is stoic, often doesn't speak, and simply does what he came to do and then leaves.

Frouros most often chooses to appears as an immense statue of gleaming silvery metal, standing between 12 feet and 200 feet tall depending on his needs. He usually does not hide his presence, but has been known to do so in order to foil covert activities. His non-metallic form is usually that of a goliath or very tall human man.
## Associated Major Deity:

[[Z[[Astere of the Night Sky-VR372\|Astere of the Night Sky]]
## Relations to other Deities:

Openly antagonistic with his brother, [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Astere/Trikar, the Shrike-VR372\|Trikar, the Shrike]]

## Origins:

Fouros and [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Astere/Trikar, the Shrike-VR372\|Trikar]] were brothers in mortal life, though they took very different paths- the Night Watchman and the Hidden Killer. Their adversarial contests and battles grew to be so epic over time that [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Astere of the Night Sky-VR372\|Astere]] was impressed. She offered to take them both into her fold- Trikar being gleefully accepting of the new power, and Frouros accepting it begrudgingly so that his brother could not escape him.

To this day, their battle is ongoing both personally and via the mortal proxies of their old stations.

## Activity:

Frouros is a very active God, physically moving from place to place to help build defenses, lay traps, keep order and above all else keep watch for extra-planar entities wishing harm on mortals- particularly his broker [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Astere/Trikar, the Shrike-VR372\|Trikar]].
