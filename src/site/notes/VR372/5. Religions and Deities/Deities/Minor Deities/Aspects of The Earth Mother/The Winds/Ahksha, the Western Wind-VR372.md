Ahksha is a [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods-VR372\|Minor God]].
###### Regional Names: 
Zephyrus ([[Thracia-VR372\|Thracia]]), The Sad Child, Melancholy, He Who Regrets

###### Domains: 
Life, Peace

###### Regional Worship: 




## Nature:

Manifesting as the smell of rain or a young boy watching a distant storm, Asksha is the calm melancholy of the changing seasons. 

Generally helpful but not very motivated, he drifts with the currents and clouds.


## Associated Major Deity:

[[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Elder Gods/The Earth Mother Irithiel-VR372\|Earth Mother]]


## Relations to other Deities:

Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/Faria, the Northern Wind-VR372\|Faria, the Northern Wind]] and [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/Estes, the Eastern Wind-VR372\|Estes, the Eastern Wind]]


## Origins:

An Old God and Child of the Earth Mother, Ahksha has drifted with the seasons since time immemorial.


## Activity:
Generally helpful but not very motivated, he drifts with the currents and clouds. He'll provide any help he can to mortals who can make a good case, but only if it is relatively low effort for him. He doesn't even bother checking to see the outcomes, or if they were lying.
