Jaraxx is a [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods-VR372\|Minor God]].

###### Regional Names: 
King of Spores, He Who Spreads Through Others

###### Domains: 
Death, Life, Nature, Order

###### Regional Worship: 

Although Jaraxx has had cults in the past, since his entrapment in the events following the [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood-VR372\|ascent]]
of [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Zatar the Ravaging Storm-VR372\|Zatar]] he has found few who would pay him homage. 



## Nature:

A tall and spindly fungal creature, Jaraxx is a creature devoted to order- his order. The controlled progression from life, to death, to decomposition and then to reanimation is his primary concern; he tries to control the process by domineering the surrounding plant and animal life, including mortals. 


## Associated Major Deity:

None. 


## Relations to other Deities:

- Though [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of the Fates/Fantis, the Weaver of Beads-VR372\|Fantis]] is angry with Jaraxx for trying to bend fate using preknowledge, he holds no grudge. 
- Although he is nowhere near powerful enough, Jaraxx still harbours plans to destroy [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Zatar the Ravaging Storm-VR372\|Zatar]] someday.
- Jaraxx is entrapped by the recuperating [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects Of Thalassus/Anuket, the Flowing Water-VR372\|Anuket]], but neither are aware of this situation.


## Origins:

Jaraxx was pulled from a pile of rotting corpses by [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of the Fates/Lady Saoirse, the Midwife-VR372\|Lady Saoirse]] before the [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood-VR372\|ascent]] of [[VR372/4. Geography, Lands and Countries/1. The Continent of the Shining Vale/2. Others/4. Cimmeria/Quidun the Wise and Beneficent-VR372\|Quidun]], which was stopped. He became active almost immediately to prevent the [[Fall of Cimmeria-VR372\|Fall of Cimmeria]], but was unsuccessful. He is the only known [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods-VR372\|Minor God]] to come from the aborted ascent of Quidun, so either it was stopped early, or we aren't yet aware of them.


## Activity:

For nearly all of his existence, Jaraxx has lived on a small island in the archipelago of the bay formed during the [[Fall of Cimmeria-VR372\|Fall of Cimmeria]]. Marooned there by [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Zatar the Ravaging Storm-VR372\|Zatar]] and unable to cross the water of the bay as [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Thalassus of the Oceans-VR372\|Thalassus]] has declared it to be sacrosanct as the recovery place for [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects Of Thalassus/Anuket, the Flowing Water-VR372\|Anuket]], Jaraxx has found himself with with an inability to cross water. He has often tried to bargain his way ashore with mortals on their vessels; some have died trying to double cross him, some have refused and been slain on the spot, but most have simply backed away from the situation. He is unsettling, after all.
