Dun is a [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods-VR372\|Minor God]].

###### Regional Names: 
Furnace Master, the Crucible (dwarven cultural), He Who Prepares
###### Domains: 
Forge, Order, War.

###### Regional Worship: 




## Nature:

Dun is a craftsman, or more accurately a crafting tool, of prodigious skill and productivity; but lacking almost entirely in creativity. It is said that Volkanus removed his own patience to craft Dun, and is thought to be the prototype of what eventually became the dwarven people.

Dun provides Volkanus with the base materials for his crafting, and occasionally crafts simple tools of legendary quality on his own.  

Dun unfolds copies of himself in a constant growing and shrinking fractal progression to travel both within and between planes; mortals who have seen this have occasionally been driven mad. 

*Dun does whatever his Master commands, and so is generally accepted by mortals as his representative.  He is always in the mortal world; if you need to find him, start by visiting the great dwarven strongholds, as he is likely at one of them.*
## Associated Major Deity:

[[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Volkanus the Maker and Reshaper of all Things-VR372\|Volkanus the Maker and Reshaper of all Things]]

## Relations to other Deities:
Dun doesn't exactly have opinions, and so it's difficult to say that he has relations to anyone but his Master. 

## Origins:

Crafted in the original Volcano in [[Thracia-VR372\|Thracia]] by [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Volkanus the Maker and Reshaper of all Things-VR372\|Volkanus]] shortly after his Ascension.

## Activity:

Constantly working, making and providing, Dun is usually in a dwarven metropolis or stronghold, but always at a forge or smeltery. Although he can't generate ideas, the things he has made are usually taken as exemplars for dwarven master smiths to copy.
