The Archon Hazidkal originally lived in the Republic of Joyous Openings, on the Fair Shore of the Land of Unspeakable Glory. After being elevated to the highest position in the Admiralty of the Republic, Hazidkal made several massive technological leaps that aided the Republic in its wars against the Ungodly Combine. The final of these was the means of traveling backwards through the strands of fate by unweaving them back to a focal node, and recreating them; altering the events of history by living them in a different way than was originally destined to happen.

But such power is addictive- using this freely to try to fix the numerous problems that inevitably appeared, one after another, farther and father backward on the weave of fate, Hazidkal found herself impossibly far in the past; [[Guradji-VR372\|Guradji]], a place with significantly less advancement and thus subject to dominion. It was here that she created the first [[Obelisks of Power-VR372\|Obelisks of Power-VR372]].

Hazidkal, empowered by her Obelisk, eventually went far enough back, and grew in power to the point that she [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood-VR372\|ascended]] and became [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/The Fates/Lachesis-VR372\|Lachesis]], thus creating the weave that she herself had unraveled and re-traversed so many times.
