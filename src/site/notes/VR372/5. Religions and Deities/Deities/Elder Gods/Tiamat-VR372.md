*A lot of power and influence can be won by wagering on Tiamat's machinations, as long as you have the inside scoop. It's not an easy thing to have, but I have a guy- let me tell you some secrets...
-Reyvan*

Tiamat is an [[VR372/5. Religions and Deities/Deities/Elder Gods-VR372\|Elder God]], imprisoned in Avernus by Asmodeus in ages past. It's not known why, or even really when. Could she escape? Possibly. Does she try? No, but she has agents try to break her out. 

It's clear that she has plans and equally clear that we don't know what they are.
