Piety is an Optional Rule from the *Mythic Odysseys of Theros* sourcebook for 5th Edition Dungeons and Dragons, published in 2020. 

Some of the Deities presented in this text are adapted from Deities found in that book, but made more suitable to the world of Irithiel; but most of the Deities have been created herein, drawing of course on the mythology of cultures found in the original and most popular campaign setting, Terra.
