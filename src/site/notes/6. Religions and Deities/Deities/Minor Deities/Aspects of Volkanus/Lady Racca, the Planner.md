Lady Racca is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Minor God]]

###### Regional Names: 
St. [[Aeonath\|Aeonath]] ([[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/The Shining Vale\|The Shining Vale]] [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/Calendar and Timekeeping/VR\|VR]] 35-492, remembered but not worshipped afterward), Berronar Truesilver (dwarven cultural), She Who Thinks and Makes
###### Domains: 
Order, Peace, War

###### Regional Worship: 
Worship of Lady Racca is often based in construction guilds in human civilization, and the reason for that construction is usually growth; so worship alongside [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Helios/Parvati, the Accepting\|Parvati]] is quite common.

When [[Aeonath\|Aeonath]] was sanctified after his death, the Cathedral in [[Haut-Revanne\|Haut-Revanne]] was already under construction.  Immediately dedicated to him, the city, [[Duchy Revanne\|Duchy Revanne]], and [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/The Shining Vale\|The Shining Vale]] saw rampant growth and construction right until the [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/History/Conquest of the Vale\|Conquest of the Vale]] in [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/Calendar and Timekeeping/VR\|VR]] 492.
## Nature:

Goddess of cities and city-building, Lady Racca is usually depicted standing tall with a compass (the drawing kind) and a ruler. This holds true in [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/The Shining Vale\|The Shining Vale]], except it is a depiction of Aeonath and he is always backlit with golden light.

## Origins:

To aid with the reconstruction of [[Thracia\|Thracia]] after Volkanus' [[6. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood\|ascent]], he removed his sense of proportion and from it crafted Lady Racca. 


## Activity:

Lady Racca operates independently from [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Volkanus the Maker and Reshaper of all Things\|Volkanus]] and serves as one of his advisors for his projects, when he chooses to listen to her. When dealing with mortals, she will often take the form of a dwarven master mason, architect or planner. She gives excellent advice, and is secretly the mind behind many of the architectural marvels of the world, thought most of them have been lost to time.
