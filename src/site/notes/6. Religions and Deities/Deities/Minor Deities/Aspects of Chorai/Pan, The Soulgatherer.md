Pan is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Minor God]]

###### Regional Names: 
He Who Crosses the Bridge
###### Domains: 
Grave

###### Regional Worship: 
None



## Nature:

Taking the form of a Ghostly grey cat, Pan wanders here and there guiding dead souls to the afterlife. He is seemingly ambivalent to his role as a psychopomp.




## Associated Major Deity:

[[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Chorai the Stillborn Dead\|Chorai the Stillborn Dead]]


## Relations to other Deities:



## Origins:

Pan arose in anticipation of the [[6. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood\|ascent]] of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Chorai the Stillborn Dead\|Chorai]].

## Activity:

Pan generally travels with a chosen mortal, extending their life and providing them with power to be able to survive the journey.
