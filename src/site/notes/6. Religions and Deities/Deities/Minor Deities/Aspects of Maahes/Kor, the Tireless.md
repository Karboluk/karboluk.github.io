Kor is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Minor God]]

###### Regional Names: 
St. Kor ([[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/The Shining Vale\|The Shining Vale]] 20-492, [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/Regions/Duchy Corwyn\|Duchy Corwyn]] PI), He Who Endures And Is Not Broken
###### Domains: 
Tempest, War

###### Regional Worship: 
There is organized worship in [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/Regions/Duchy Corwyn\|Duchy Corwyn]], but the Goliaths of [[The Dal\|The Dal]] and [[The Wastes of Subdurni\|The Wastes of Subdurni]] venerate his ideals alongside [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Maahes of Shield and Blade\|The Fury]].

## Nature:

Kor was a long suffering human man, part of the original troupe of adventurers responsible for scouting [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/The Shining Vale\|The Shining Vale]] after the [[Fall of Cimmeria\|Fall of Cimmeria]] and making it safe for settlement. He carries the weight of these adventures, and the heavy events that occurred, into his new duties.

## Origins:

It seems that [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Maahes of Shield and Blade\|Maahes]] was displeased at the treatment of Kor by [[Callamus the Righteous\|Callamus the Righteous]] during the original settlement of [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/The Shining Vale\|The Shining Vale]]. As Kor headed east, he encountered tribal people and shared his experience, attitude and what could generously be called his philosophy- [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Maahes of Shield and Blade\|Maahes]] was secretly present for several of these and decided that this activity would be beneficial in the race for influence against [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Zatar the Ravaging Storm\|Zatar]]. On one fateful night, he granted Kor divinity- it is not known if Kor was willing or informed of this ahead of time.




## Activity:

Regardless of what he may think of his new role, Kor has taken to it with diligence. Teaching those willing to learn, and able to survive, his ways, new orders have sprung up following his teaching. These include:

- [[The Way of Mithril Skin\|The Way of Mithril Skin]]
- [[The Bloodless\|The Bloodless]]
- [[The Sisters of Stone and Axe\|The Sisters of Stone and Axe]]
- [[The Madres\|The Madres]]
