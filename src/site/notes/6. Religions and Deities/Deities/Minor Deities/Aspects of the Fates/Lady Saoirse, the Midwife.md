## Nature:

Lady Saoirse is depicted with cradled arms and a smile, carrying no articles of faith. She presides over childbirth and midwifery, leading to much faster population gains and much smaller families than if her influence didn't exist.

## Origins:

Lady Saoirse may have existed before, but she was first noticed by the pantheon at large at the birth of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Other Minor Deities/Jaraxx, the Rot\|Jaraxx]]; having drawn him from a pile of corpses and cleaned him up, she then moved on to other duties with very little comment.


## Activity:

Lady Saoirse is always quietly working, teaching midwives, and fulfilling her calling with no fuss, muss or bother. She only rears her head up when something she loves is threatened- she is a mother to so many, after all.
