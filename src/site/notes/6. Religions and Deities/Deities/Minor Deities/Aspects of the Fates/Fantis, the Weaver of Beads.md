Fantis is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Minor God]]
###### Regional Names: 
The Spider, Bringer of Sorrow, She Who Places Burdens Onto Our Lives
###### Domains: 
Knowledge, Order

###### Regional Worship: 
Fantis is more appeased than worshipped by mortals. They want to keep her ministrations far, far away. Fantis does, however, provide services for [[Oracles\|Oracles]].

## Nature:

A crystalline glass woman, giant in size, with spider-like limbs protruding from her back and over her shoulders, Fantis is an imposing figure. She is however, relatively non-threatening unless provoked, and has a calm, logical demeanor. 
 
Fantis works tirelessly at her table, pulling threads from the weave of fate out of the air and placing weights shaped like slotted disks upon them. Thus spread the burdens of the living.


## Associated Major Deity:
[[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/The Fates/Lachesis\|Lachesis]]

## Relations to other Deities:

- [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Astere/Paratiritis, the Seeing\|Paratiritis, the Seeing]] is a trusted friend and useful compatriot for Fantis.
- Fantis is cordial with [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Chorai/The Legion of the Dead\|The Legion of the Dead]], who knows her from before Evette ascended.
- Because [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Other Minor Deities/Jaraxx, the Rot\|Jaraxx]] tried to use knowledge of the weave of fate to his advantage, Fantis has refused to reduce his burdens. Whether she added them originally isn't known, but seems likely. 


## Origins:

Given the manner of ascent of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/The Fates/Lachesis\|Lachesis]], it's likely that Fantis was created or born to serve a specific need or change at some point. We can't know what that was, or if it is still relevant, but she serves her Mistress well and so continues to exist.


## Activity:

Fantis can't spare time to leave her table, but she can move it. She has been found in many places over time; usually in places of oracular prophecy. At last notice, she was in the [[Great Temple in the Sky\|Great Temple in the Sky]] where the [[5. History and Myths/Other Places/Obelisks of Power\|Obelisk of Divination]] can be found. 

Angering or slighting Fantis or [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/The Fates/Lachesis\|Lachesis]] usually ends up with more weights set on your thread. Those who find ways to add those weights themselves quickly become the focus of Fantis' ire.
