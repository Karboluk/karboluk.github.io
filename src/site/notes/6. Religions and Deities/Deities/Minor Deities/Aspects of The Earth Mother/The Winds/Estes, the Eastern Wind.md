Estes is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Minor God]]
###### Regional Names: 
Eurus ([[Thracia\|Thracia]]), The Sky Serpent, Feathered Dragon, Ouroboros, He Who Brings Life
###### Domains: 
Life, Light, Nature

###### Regional Worship: 
Estes is worshipped heavily in some civilizations, particularly in places that have smaller settlements and haven't yet found the "truth and art" part of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Helios the Radiant Truth\|Helios]] as valuable as the "please bring a gentle spring with rain and sun so we don't starve". Is often depicted as the lover of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/The Fates/Clotho\|Clotho]].



## Nature:

A Feathered Serpent, coiling and endless, Estes is the fire writ upon the morning sky and the mist of the high clouds.


Estes is not very active, in terms of interfacing with mortals personally. But of all [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/The Winds\|the Winds]], he takes his remit the most seriously, and works with diligence to bring fertility and growth to all places, usually in the wake of his siblings.

## Relations to other Deities:

Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/Douma, the Southern Wind\|Douma, the Southern Wind]], and [[6. ## Origins:

An Old God and Child of the Earth Mother, Estes has graced the world since time immemorial.



## Activity:

Estes travels constantly, renewing the lands of his mother and providing the opportunity for growth. He is somewhat forgotten in "more civilized" lands, but sometimes farmers keep totems, and island folk rarely go a year without celebrating for him.
