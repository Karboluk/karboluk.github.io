*Faria is an example of what a god should be, in my opinion; she is engaged in the mortal world, opinionated, capricious, merciless and just free enough with her power that mortals have a positive opinion of her- so they keep coming back for more.
-Reyvan*

Faria is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Minor God]]
###### Regional Names: 
Boreas ([[Thracia\|Thracia]]), The Giantess, She Who Usurps
###### Domains: 
Tempest, Trickery

###### Regional Worship: 
Some veneration among giants, but not worship.



## Nature:

Faria has a whimsical nature, endless patience for activities and people she enjoys, and an implacable thirst for vengeance- and also a strongly cruel sense of humour.

The most active of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/The Winds\|the Winds]], Faria will interface with mortals fairly regularly in her usual Storm Giant form; given how few records describe this, it's likely that they never knew she was a Goddess. 




## Associated Major Deity:
[[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Elder Gods/The Earth Mother Irithiel\|The Earth Mother Irithiel]]

## Relations to other Deities:
Sister to [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/Ahksha, the Western Wind\|Ahksha, the Western Wind]], [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/Douma, the Southern Wind\|Douma, the Southern Wind]], and [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/Estes, the Eastern Wind\|Estes, the Eastern Wind]].


## Origins:

An Old God and Child of the Earth Mother, Faria has roamed the world since time immemorial.


## Activity:

Faria engages in what she describes as "projects", which can vary in scope and scale wildly. Helping a settlement be established, removing a problematic mountain, restoring a rightful heir, or torturing someone she believes deserves it- being so old, her patience means that she will pursue her chosen goal relentlessly for centuries if required.
