Douma is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Minor God]]
###### Regional Names: 
Notus ([[Thracia\|Thracia]]), Hunger, the Swarm, She Who Consumes

###### Domains: 
Death, Tempest, War

###### Regional Worship: 


## Nature:

Douma is a creature of consumption, desolation and malice. 

She roams the world, consuming what is in her path- occasionally she will steer towards a particular direction, which has lead to some cult-like worship.


## Associated Major Deity:
[[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Elder Gods/The Earth Mother Irithiel\|The Earth Mother Irithiel]]

## Relations to other Deities:
Sister to [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/Ahksha, the Western Wind\|Ahksha, the Western Wind]], [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/Estes, the Eastern Wind\|Estes, the Eastern Wind]] and [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/Faria, the Northern Wind\|Faria, the Northern Wind]]


## Origins:

An Old God and Child of the Earth Mother, Douma has plagued the world since time immemorial.


## Activity:
Douma is not mindless, and can be petitioned to avoid an area, or to go to one. Some cults have formed around her, and some mortals, especially agrarian or druidic mortals, provide her offerings in high places to entice her away from their lands.
