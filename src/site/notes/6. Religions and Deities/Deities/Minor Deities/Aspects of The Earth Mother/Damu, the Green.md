Damu is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Minor God]]

###### Regional Names: 
Demeter ([[Thracia\|Thracia]]), The Green, The Wellspring, The Sower, She Who Plants Now and Eats Tomorrow
###### Domains: 
Life, Nature

###### Regional Worship: 

Damu is worshiped under many names, in many places, and in ways that mortals don't even understand that they are worshiping her.



## Nature:

Damu is a calm and patient goddess, who understands that all of her work is for the benefit of others. She brings life to plants, to bring life to animals, to bring life to mortals, to bring life to the New Gods. She has never once complained that she can never benefit from this process.

Damu is always active in the world, as the work of growth is never-ending.

## Origins:


Damu is probably the first of the [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Minor Gods]], though the actual order between her and [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of The Earth Mother/The Winds/The Winds\|The Winds]] is unknown. Due to age, no record of [[6. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood\|ascent]] exists; and given her working it's unlikely that mortals existed on the world at the time. This makes it likely that she was created by the [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Elder Gods/The Earth Mother Irithiel\|Earth Mother]] directly, rather than brought to divinity from another state.


## Activity:

Damu works the lands, sowing seeds of every plant and animal, ensuring crops, litters, herds, towns and civilizations replenish themselves in the endless cycle.
