Maahes is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Gods\|Major God]]

###### Regional Names: 
Polemos ([[Thracia\|Thracia]]), Hargon ([[Sendria\|Sendria]]), The Fury ([[The Dal\|The Dal]], [[Subdurni\|Subdurni]])

###### Domains: 
Forge, Life, Light, Order, War.

###### Regional Worship: 
The [[6. Religions and Deities/Religions/Battlesworn\|Battlesworn]] ([[Thracia\|Thracia]]), [[The Brotherhood of Mithril Skin\|The Brotherhood of Mithril Skin]] ([[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/The Shining Vale\|The Shining Vale]]) by way of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Maahes/Kor, the Tireless\|Kor]], The Goliath of [[The Dal\|The Dal]] and the Hobgoblins and Goliath both of [[The Wastes of Subdurni\|The Wastes of Subdurni]]



## Nature:

Maahes lives for the glory of battle, preferring honorable combat and challenge over brutality and deceptive tactics. He does not explicitly champion the causes of good, civilization, or justice, but because those causes more often lead to glory than chaos, he has become intertwined with them.

Maahes reaches out to the mortals under his influence in visions, dreams, and rituals. A call to greater fury, a rejection of the fear of death, a compulsion to take up arms and do what they know must be done, in the full light of everyone involved; these are some of the ways mortals hear the call of [[The War-Horn of Maahes\|The War-Horn of Maahes]]. In particular, he tries his best to court anyone he thinks is susceptible to the path of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Zatar the Ravaging Storm\|Zatar]].

## Associated Minor Deities/Aspect Deities:

[[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Maahes/Kor, the Tireless\|Kor, the Tireless]], Aspect of Endurance

## Relations to other Deities:

Maahes bears a particular resentment to the relatively new god [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Zatar the Ravaging Storm\|Zatar the Ravaging Storm]]. Although Maahes would have struck [[Cimmeria\|Cimmeria]] down, had he had the chance, the duplicitous way it was done and the harm it has caused [[The Eastern Continent\|The Eastern Continent]] aggrieves him. 

## Origins:

Maahes originated in what is now [[Thracia\|Thracia]] during the invasion of [[Apophis\|Apophis]], which triggered the Sipparan War. Defeating Apophis after his [[6. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood\|Ascent to Godhood]], Maahes's sword-stroke left the land scarred- they call it the [[Thracia#Tiger'sStripe\|Tiger's Stripe]] now, with a wide swath of desecrated land beyond it. 

I remember that Maahes was much more directly involved in the mortal world back then, and shaped what has become the Thracian civilization directly; I believe he wanted to prepare them to fight the undead menace in the [[Haunted Land\|Haunted Land]]. But, as many of the early gods discovered, interfering simply caused problems; the belligerence he gave the Thracians has caused much civil war and strife, leaving the Haunted Land still unconquered, and his physical presence shattered their land into the archipelago we find today. 


## Activity:

Maahes like war, there's no subtlety and nothing mor to it. He encourages it, but with all the normal niceties of a formal theatre of combat. Simple, but effective, given how easy mortals are to influence to violence. 


## Desired Behaviour in Followers:

Training, war, perhaps some light skirmishing before lunch, then advocating for military funding before having another nice war before the end of day. No attacks after dark, because that is cowardly; unless against a vastly superior foe, of course, in which case it's "tactically advantageous". There's nothing like a creed filled with self-serving rationale.


### [[50. Glossary and Administrative/Player Character Options and Rules/Rules/Piety - Optional Rule\|Piety]] Benefits:
You increase your piety score to Maahes when you expand the god’s influence in the world in a concrete way through acts such as these:

- Achieving a great victory
- Overcoming long odds honorably
- Defeating a skilled foe in single combat, particularly a follower of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Zatar the Ravaging Storm\|Zatar]].
- Winning a great feat of strength or skill

Your piety score to Maahes decreases if you diminish Maahes’s influence in the world, contradict his ideals, or make him look weak and cowardly through acts such as these:

- Showing cowardice in battle
- Besting an honorable foe through deceit
- Harming innocents or non-combatants

-**Maahes’s Devotee** _Piety 3+ Maahes trait_. As a devotee of Maahes, you have earned his favor through victories won in his name. You can cast _compelled duel_ with this trait a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once), or with spell slots. You regain all expended free uses when you finish a long rest. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell. 

-**Maahes’s Votary** _Piety 10+ Maahes trait_. You can cast _crusader’s mantle_ with this trait or with spell slots. You regain your free use when you finish a long rest. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell. In addition, you have advantage on saving throws against being frightened. 

-**Maahes’s Disciple** _Piety 25+ Maahes trait_. As a bonus action, you can call on Maahes’s blessing, gaining the following benefit for 1 minute or until you are incapacitated: creatures within 30 feet of you can’t gain advantage on attack rolls against you. Once you use this trait, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest. 

-**Champion of Victory** _Piety 50+ Maahes trait_. You can increase your Strength or Charisma score by 2 and also increase your maximum for that score by 2.
