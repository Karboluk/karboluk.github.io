Atropos is a [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Gods\|Major God]]

###### Regional Names: 
The Crone (Common), The Old Bag (derogatory, but never punished), She who severs all threads.
###### Domains: 
Arcana, Death, Grave, Knowledge

###### Regional Worship: 
As with the other fates, specific worship of Atropos is rare; but there are several religious orders in [[Thracia\|Thracia]] and beyond that strive to mercilessly eradicate the undead and those who create them; when not busy, they often serve as judges and executioners. Unusually for immortals, some elves of cruel persuasion find themselves in this calling.



## Nature:

Atropos is the heartless being that ends all mortal life, the final arbiter of your story and the one who knows all that came before. The Old Crone, she is a haunting, terrifying and comforting figure all in one.


## Associated Minor Deities/Aspect Deities:

Sister to [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/The Fates/Clotho\|Clotho]] and [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/The Fates/Lachesis\|Lachesis]]

## Relations to other Deities:

It is assumed that Atropos will strongly oppose [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Chorai the Stillborn Dead\|Chorai]], once they become active.

## Origins:

I think it would be disrespectful to discuss the beginning of the One Who Ends, and so I shall defer to the imagination of the reader.


## Activity:

Almost everything ends. She must be busy, or have a lot of help.

## Desired Behaviour in Followers:

Be relentless in ending that which needs to end, even more so for something that has come back from after its time. Shun the resurrected, and never participate in such things yourself. However, mourn, celebrate, and respect the properly dead. Commune with their spirits if you wish, but be civil; they have obeyed the order of things.

Remember the lessons of your life. 
### [[50. Glossary and Administrative/Player Character Options and Rules/Rules/Piety - Optional Rule\|Piety]] Benefits:

You increase your piety score to Atropos when you expand the goddess’s influence in the world in a concrete way through acts such as these:

- Preventing a person from living beyond their time
- Destroying an Undead creature in Atropos’ name
- Using past history to predict an important outcome.

Your piety score to Atropos decreases if you diminish Atropos or the Fates’ influence in the world, contradict their ideals, or make her look weak or unimportant through acts like these:

- Creating or controlling an undead creature, or allowing one to exist.
- Resurrecting a creature, or allowing a resurrection to happen.
- Failing to learn from past examples.

**Atropos’s Devotee** _Piety 3+ Atropos trait_. As a devotee of Atropos, you have earned her favor through wise acts in her name. You can cast _inflict wounds_ with this trait once per long rest and with spell slots. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell. 

**Atropos’s Votary** _Piety 10+ Atropos trait_. You can cast _ray of enfeeblement_ You can cast _inflict wounds_ with this trait once per long rest and with spell slots. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell. In addition, you are immune to effects which would reduce your maximum hit points or change your age. 

**Atropos’s Disciple** _Piety 25+ Atropos trait_. As a bonus action, you can call on Atropos’s blessing and cast _blight_. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell. Once you use this trait, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest. 

**Paragon of Experience** _Piety 50+ Atropos trait_. You can increase your Constitution or Wisdom score by 2 and also increase your maximum for that score by 2.
