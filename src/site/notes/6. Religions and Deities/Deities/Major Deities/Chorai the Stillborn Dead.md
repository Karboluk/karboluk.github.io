## Nature:

Chorai is the nascent goddess of Death, Undeath and the Undying. Prevented from emerging in full, this new goddess is in a state of torpor; only time will tell if they grow enough to accept any true position in the pantheon.

As of latest reckoning, Chorai has not yet interfaced with mortals, so no understanding of her exists at large. Her existence is known, however, to a few learned individuals.

## Origins:

Having experimented with necromantic magic for decades, and having learned from [[5. History and Myths/Cimmeria/Quidun the Wise and Benificent\|Quidun the Wise and Benificent]] in his demi-lich state following the [[Fall of Cimmeria\|Fall of Cimmeria]], the [[Sendria\|Sendrian]] wizard [[Soza Hakade\|Soza Hakade]] attempted to use the [[5. History and Myths/Other Places/Obelisks of Power\|Obelisks of Power]] in the way that [[5. History and Myths/Cimmeria/Quidun the Wise and Benificent\|Quidun]] had originally planned, which was to [[6. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood\|Ascend]]. Altering the Obelisks to draw their power from afar, she amassed magical power while also amassing political power as the head of the [[Magesterium Panopticon\|Magesterium Panopticon]] in the country of [[Sendria\|Sendria]]. 

Her plans were rushed, and eventually thwarted, by a group of adventurers known as [[The Seekers\|The Seekers]]. Enough of the process had occurred, however, that her [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Gods\|Aspect Deities]] had already emerged. Their continued presence in the world is the largest piece of evidence that Chorai still has a role to play.

Given the unexpected ascension of the God [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Rrakash of Tears and Torment\|Rrakash]] at this time, it has been theorized that the divine energy was divided between them, slowing the ascension of both and giving them time to withdraw before calamity could occur.
