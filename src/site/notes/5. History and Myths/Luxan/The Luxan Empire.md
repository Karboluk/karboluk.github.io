The Luxan Empire existed in the distant past in [[The Eastern Continent\|The Eastern Continent]], having fallen long before the elves of [[The Woad\|The Woad]] arrived. Their sites are clearly constructed on intersections of lines of power. From the archeological evidence, they appear to have been mostly Tabaxi, with with a sizable slave-caste of dwarves.
