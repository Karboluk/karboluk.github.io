Isthariel was a powerful Potentate in the [[Cimmerian Empire\|Cimmerian Empire]], using her magical prowess to dominate a large section of the city and suppress any potential challengers for almost 200 years. During this time, she managed to defeat her only equal, [[5. History and Myths/Cimmeria/Quidun the Wise and Benificent\|Quidun]], by using guile to waylay him long enough to create an [[5. History and Myths/Other Places/Obelisks of Power\|Obelisk]] that channeled large amounts of power to herself (by tapping what is now the active volcano at [[Uxtatli\|Uxtatli]]), allowing her great resilience, regenerative power, and magical energy.
 
Isthariel was eventually exiled by a cabal of lesser wizards and divine practitioners, forcing her to flee from her own palace onto the plane of [[Limbo\|Limbo]], where she spent the timeless days preparing; it is there that she encountered agents of an [[Elder God\|Elder God]], [[Orryx\|Orryx]], who told her the story of the rise of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Thalassus of the Oceans\|Thalassus]] and the fall of the ancient culture of [[Bassaria\|Bassaria]]. 

This information taught Isthariel that there was a way to gain even greater power and get revenge on her foes. In pursuing this goal over the sleepless timescape of the plane, the merciless Isthariel lost any remaining vestige of kindness or restraint under the sanity-draining tutelage of [[Orryx\|Orryx]]’s acolytes. Shortly after this she ascended fully and returned to the world as the God [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Major Deities/Zatar the Ravaging Storm\|Zatar]].
 
Her wrath was the cause of the fall of the [[Cimmerian Empire\|Cimmerian Empire]], as she systematically destroyed all those who opposed her, and anyone around them. Her final act during her [[6. Religions and Deities/Ascent to Godhood\|Ascent to Godhood]] was to cast the [[City of Cimmeria\|City of Cimmeria]] down the ground from the heights, burying it into the landscape and creating a large bay North of what is now [[Port Rain\|Port Rain]], and vertically approximately above [[Applewood\|Applewood]]. 

Several lesser gods, such as [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Other Minor Deities/Jaraxx, the Rot\|Jaraxx, the Rot]], attempted to intervene, so once Cimmeria was in ruins, Zatar turned her eyes to the gods as the new source of her unbridled, raging, insanity.
