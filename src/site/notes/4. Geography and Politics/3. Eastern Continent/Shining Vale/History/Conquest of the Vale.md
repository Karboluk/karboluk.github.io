The Conquest of the Vale in [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/Calendar and Timekeeping/VR\|VR]] 492 resulted in the destruction of [[Duchy Revanne\|Duchy Revanne]], the erasure of the history of the dwarves in [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/The Shining Vale\|The Shining Vale]], the sealing of the cities and abandonment of the towns in [[Duchy Redhill\|Duchy Redhill]] and widescale warfare in [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/Regions/Duchy Corwyn\|Duchy Corwyn]]. [[Duchy Truscia\|Duchy Truscia]] had been warned ahead of time in an event called [[Il Patto Del Diavolo\|Il Patto Del Diavolo]], or the Devil's Bargain, and so they surrendered immediately and were completely spared.

The Conquest was lead by [[Callamus the Righteous\|Callamus the Righteous]], leading an army of Celestials named [[The Golden Fist\|The Golden Fist]]. The Fist were left in charge after Callamus departed, with the Celestials leaving, instead drawing recruits from the mortal citizens. 

The Conquest, historically framed as a liberation from Monarchy, resulted in the erasure of the religion of [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects of Volkanus/Lady Racca, the Planner\|St. Aeonath]].
