Vale Reckoning (VR) is the calendar system used in the Shining Vale during the time of the Revannian Monarchy. 

VR 1 began at the coronation of King Aeonath the Civilizer, leader of the heroes who pacified the Vale, in the settlement of Haut-Revanne.

VR 1 corresponds to [[AY\|AY]] 

After the [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/History/Conquest of the Vale\|Conquest of the Vale]] VR stopped being monitored, with the entire period being referred to as [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/Calendar and Timekeeping/PI\|PI]] (Post-Invasion)
