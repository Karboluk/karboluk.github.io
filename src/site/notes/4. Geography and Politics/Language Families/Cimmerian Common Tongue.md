The [[Cimmerian Empire\|Cimmerian Empire]] used a specific language for their official state communications, though local areas may have used a separate language for day-to-day duties, particularly if they were not a predominantly human culture. This language is still the common tongue for any of the former colonies or inheritor countries after the [[Fall of Cimmeria\|Fall of Cimmeria]], including those listed below.

- [[Sendria\|Sendria]]
- [[4. Geography and Politics/6. Central Continent/Hellenburg/Hellenburg\|Hellenburg]]
- [[4. Geography and Politics/3. Eastern Continent/Shining Vale/The Shining Vale\|The Shining Vale]]
- [[Port Rain\|Port Rain]]

It is a minority language in the following countries, as either it was never the official language, or if cultural tongues are taken over. It will still likely be understood by some fraction of the people.


- [[4. Geography and Politics/6. Central Continent/Lausanne/Lausanne\|Lausanne]]
- [[Killiburry\|Killiburry]]
- [[Uxtatli\|Uxtatli]]
