As [[Bassaria\|Bassaria]] was the first kingdom of mortals, they did attempt to formalise a consistent language. Not spoken widely, Bassarian common was used mostly for court and priestly records as written accounts were a valuable commodity. Many of the surviving texts are located in [[The Aethenium\|The Aethenium]] as they were collected by, and often written by, Apkallu Who Reads, who became [[6. Religions and Deities/Deities/Minor Deities/Aspects Of Thalassus/Aethenius, the Scholar\|Aethenius, the Scholar]].

After the [[Sundering of the Lands\|Sundering of the Lands]], Bassarian common was still used in by the functionaries in the city of [[Bassaria\|Bassaria]], but eventually declined after the city fell.
