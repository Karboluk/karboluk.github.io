Faria is a Minor God

Regional Names:

Boreas (Thracia), The Giantess, She Who Usurps

Domains:

Tempest, Trickery

Regional Worship:

Some veneration among giants, but not worship.

Nature:

Faria has a whimsical nature, endless patience for activities and people she enjoys, and an implacable thirst for vengeance- and also a strongly cruel sense of humour.

The most active of the Winds, Faria will interface with mortals fairly regularly in her usual Storm Giant form; given how few records describe this, it's likely that they never knew she was a Goddess.

Associated Major Deity:

The Earth Mother Irithiel

Relations to other Deities:

Sister to Ahksha, the Western Wind, Douma, the Southern Wind, and Estes, the Eastern Wind.

Origins:

An Old God and Child of the Earth Mother, Faria has roamed the world since time immemorial.

Activity:

Faria engages in what she describes as "projects", which can vary in scope and scale wildly. Helping a settlement be established, removing a problematic mountain, restoring a rightful heir, or torturing someone she believes deserves it- being so old, her patience means that she will pursue her chosen goal relentlessly for centuries if required.
