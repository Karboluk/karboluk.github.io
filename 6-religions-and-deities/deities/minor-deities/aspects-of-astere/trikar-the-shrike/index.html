Trikar is a Minor God

Regional Names:

Janus (Thracia), He Who Kills With No Warning

Domains:

Trickery, War

Regional Worship:

Trikar tends to be venerated by very few civilized folk, as any church or cult to him is very quickly stamped out by the more civilized folk. Individual followers exist though, and are often found at the heart of a thieves' guild or similar organization enabling and blessing their activities. How they come to know of and worship Trikar is unknown and likely down to the individual.

Nature:

Trikar's outlook on mortals is fairly simple: they are prey to be taken down. Unlike Chiriki who epitomizes the hunting of beasts for food or glory, Trikar's motivation is simply the act itself.

Trikar stokes the hearts of evil mortals, or those with the capacity for evil, drawing them closer to his mindset.

Associated Major Deity:

Astere of the Night Sky

Relations to other Deities:

Trikar is in an openly antagonistic relationship with his brother, Frouros, the Watchman.

Origins:

Trikar and Frouros were brothers in mortal life, though they took very different paths- the Hidden Killer and the Night Watchman. Their adversarial contests and battles grew to be so epic over time that Astere was impressed. She offered to take them both into her fold- Trikar being gleefully accepting of the new power, and Frouros accepting it begrudgingly so that his brother could not escape him.

To this day, their battle is ongoing both personally and via the mortal proxies of their old stations.

Activity:

Trikar operates very much as devils do, offering power for services. Those whom he courts may not understand that those services just bring them further under his sway. When he is described, it is, unhelpfully, as "a person in the room that could not be seen".
