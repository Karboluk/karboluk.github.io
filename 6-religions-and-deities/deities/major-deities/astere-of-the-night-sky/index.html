Astere is a Major God

Regional Names:

Selene, The Moonmaiden, She Who Sees What You Hide, Lady Dark

Domains:

Twilight,Trickery,War

Regional Worship:

Nightwatchers (Thracia), Sisters of the Moon (The Shining Vale)

Nature:

Astere, Goddess of the Night Sky, sees everything that happens in the darkness when Helios leaves the world. She is sometimes, a protector of the weak, sometimes a keeper of secrets, sometimes the hider of secrets, but always a lover of mystery.

Astere, like most Major Gods, does not interact directly with the world for fear of damaging it. But she is very active through her agents, her aspect deities, and those of other Major Gods friendly towards her and her goals.

Associated Minor Deities/Aspect Deities:

Frouros, the Watchman, Aspect of Vigilance
Paratiritis, the Seeing, Aspect of Observation
Trikar, the Shrike, Aspect of the Assassin

Relations to other Deities:

Astere is assumed to be the counterpart of Helios, but the two do not have an obvious relationship. Lady Dark, ever mysterious, is difficult to shed light on.

Origins:

Try as I might, Astere's origins are somewhat mysterious, and there is no written record nor cultural memory of her origin or any disaster her Ascent to Godhood may have caused.

She was first noticed after the Sundering of the Lands in ancient Bassaria; prior to that event the sky had always been the domain of Helios- with so much more territory to cover, he could no longer illuminate everyone and when he first moved away and the sky grew dark, Astere could clearly be seen. It could be that she had always been there, or that she moved to fill the void of Helios' absence.

Desired Behaviour in Followers:

Piety Benefits:

You increase your piety score to Astere when you expand the goddess’s influence in the world in a concrete way through acts such as these:

  • Keeping a dangerous secret despite personal cost
  • Revealing a critical truth at an important moment
  • Mediating a major dispute or conflict
  • Aiding the weak or impeding the strong

Your piety score to Astere decreases if you diminish Astere’s influence in the world, upset the balance of power, or make her look biased or petty through acts such as these:

  • Revealing a secret that should remain unknown
  • Selfishly refusing to share information
  • Showing favoritism toward any other god or group
  • Furthering the aims of another god in defiance of Astere’s will

-Astere’s Devotee Piety 3+ Astere trait. As a devotee of Astere, you have proven yourself a devotee of reality’s mysteries and laws. You can cast detect magic with this trait a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once), or with a spell slot. You regain all expended free uses when you finish a long rest. In addition, you know the mage hand cantrip. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for these spells. 
-Astere’s Votary Piety 10+ Astere trait. You can cast detect thoughts once with this trait, or with spell slots, requiring no material components. You regain your expended free use when you finish a long rest. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for this spell. In addition, you have advantage on saving throws against being charmed. 
-Astere’s Disciple Piety 25+ Astere trait. As a true champion of Astere, you are aware of everything that goes on around you. You gain 30' blindsight.
-Champion of the Night Piety 50+ Astere trait. You can increase your Constitution or Intelligence score by 2 and increase your maximum for that score by 2.
