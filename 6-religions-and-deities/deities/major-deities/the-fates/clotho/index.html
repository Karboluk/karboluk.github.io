Clotho is a Major God

Regional Names:

The Spinner (common), The Maiden, (common)

Domains:

Life, Peace

Regional Worship:

Venerated nearly everywhere by people, particularly midwives and farmers with breeding flocks. Is often, but not always, depicted as the lover of Estes, the Eastern Wind in fertility cults.

Nature:

Clotho creates new lives and is very invested in nurturing them. She knows that they will end, but mourns each and every one. She is a tranquil being of harmony, peace and creation.

Associated Minor Deities/Aspect Deities:

Lady Saoirse, the Midwife, Aspect of Childbirth

Relations to other Deities:

Sister to Lachesis and Atropos. Presumably violently angry at Chorai and Zatar, but you wouldn't know it as she's so damned wholesome.

Origins:

Did she originate, or is she constantly being born? It would fit, surely. Otherwise, no clue.

Activity:

Well.....babies. I think that should suffice as proof.

Desired Behaviour in Followers:

Encourage life and love, grow communities and collective support of mothers and babies.

Piety Benefits:

You increase your piety score to Clotho when you expand the god’s influence in the world in a concrete way through acts such as these:

  • Helping a new life enter the world in a difficult situation
  • Helping a peaceful community gain the resources they need to grow, in Clotho’s name
  • Safeguarding the lineage of an important person.

Your piety score to Clotho decreases if you diminish Clotho or the Fates’ influence in the world, contradict their ideals, or make her look weak or unimportant through acts like these:

  • Killing, or allowing the unnecessary death of, children, the elderly and particularly mothers.
  • Encouraging war for the sake of war
  • Causing or encouraging acts which will harm the general populace, like a coup or excess taxation.

-Clotho’s Devotee Piety 3+ Clotho trait. As a devotee of Clotho, you have earned her favor through generous acts in her name. You can cast cure wounds with this trait once per long rest, or with spell slots. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

-Clotho’s Votary Piety 10+ Clotho trait. You can cast create food and water with this trait once per long rest with this trait, or with spell slots. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell. In addition, those who eat the food you create with this spell or goodberry are immune to the poisoned and diseased conditions until they finish a long rest.

-Clotho’s Disciple Piety 25+ Clotho trait. As a bonus action, you can call on Clotho’s blessing and cast aura of life. Once you use this trait, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest.

-Embodiment of Giving Piety 50+ Clotho trait. You can increase your Wisdom or Charisma score by 2 and also increase your maximum for that score by 2.
