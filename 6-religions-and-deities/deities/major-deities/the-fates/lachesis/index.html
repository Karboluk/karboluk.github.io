Lachesis is a Major God

Regional Names:

The Grand Weaver (common, not to be confused with her spider-girl lackey), The Mother (not uncommon, but not usually used.

Domains:

Arcana, Knowledge, Trickery

Regional Worship:

Offerings are commonly made everywhere, but more to ward away her attention than to gain her benefit.

Nature:

Lachesis is a master designer, patron of those who make intricate plans, and those who seek foreknowledge. As such, her adherents include more than just clerics, but also spies, inventors, politicians and criminals.

Associated Minor Deities/Aspect Deities:

Fantis, the Weaver of Beads

Relations to other Deities:

Sister to Clotho and Atropos.

Origins:

Well, where does one begin? At the end, I think. Here is the currently known truth, but as you'll see- it may not will have always be untrue for long. The Tale of Hazidkal

Activity:

How would you even begin to tell? And if you did, is it because she made you do it?

Desired Behaviour in Followers:

Generally serve as diviners. A conduit to the mortal realm is a useful thing for the Grand Weaver to have

Piety Benefits:

You increase your piety score to Lachesis when you expand the goddess’s influence in the world in a concrete way through acts such as these:

- Defeating a creature that has stepped out of its place, particularly in a way that punishes them for all to see
 - Repairing a significant wound dealt to destiny by the gods’ ambitions
 - Bringing a reluctant person to accept the destiny given to them

Your piety score to Lachesis decreases if you diminish Lachesis or the Fates’ influence in the world, contradict their ideals, or make her look weak or unimportant through acts like these:
 - Undoing a deserved punishment or curse suffered by another creature
 - Changing the fate of a creature, even if following a God’s command
 - Assisting a creature in exploiting destiny, or exploiting it yourself
 
 -Lachesis’s Devotee  Piety 3+ Lachesis trait.  As a devotee of Lachesis, you have earned her favor through clever acts in her name. You can cast comprehend languages with this trait a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once) or using spell slots. You regain all free uses when you complete a long rest, and Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for this spell.
 
 -Lachesis’s Votary Piety 10+ Lachesis trait. You can cast detect thoughts with this trait or with spell slots. You regain your free use when you complete a long rest. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for this spell.  In addition, you are immune to effects which would detect your mind, your creature type or alignment, or read your thoughts.
 
 -Lachesis’s Disciple Piety 25+ Lachesis trait. As a bonus action, you can call on Lachesis’s blessing and cast divination. Once you use this trait, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest.
 
 -Paragon of Knowledge Piety 50+ Lachesis trait. You can increase your Intelligence or Wisdom score by 2 and also increase your maximum for that score by 2.
