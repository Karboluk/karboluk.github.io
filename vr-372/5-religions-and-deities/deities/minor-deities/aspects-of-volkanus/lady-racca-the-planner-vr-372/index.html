Lady Racca, the Planner-VR372

The influence of Lady Racca is responsible for most of the beauty that the disciples of Helios take credit for.
-Reyvan

Lady Racca is a Minor God.

Regional Names:

St. Aeonath (The Shining Vale 35-current), Berronar Truesilver (dwarven cultural), She Who Thinks and Makes

Domains:

Order, Peace, War

Regional Worship:

Worship of Lady Racca is often based in construction guilds in human civilization, and the reason for that construction is usually growth; so worship alongside Parvati is quite common.

When Aeonath was sanctified after his death, the Cathedral in Haut-Revanne was already under construction. Immediately dedicated to him, the city, Duchy Revanne, and The Shining Vale saw a rampant increase in growth and construction.

Nature:

Goddess of cities and city-building, Lady Racca is usually depicted standing tall with a compass (the drawing kind) and a ruler. This holds true in The Shining Vale, except it is a depiction of Aeonath and he is always backlit with golden light.

Associated Major Deity:

Volkanus the Maker and Reshaper of all Things

Relations to other Deities:

Origins:

To aid with the reconstruction of Thracia after Volkanus' ascent, he removed his sense of proportion- and from it crafted Lady Racca.

Activity:

Lady Racca operates independently from Volkanus and serves as one of his advisors for his projects; when he chooses to listen to her. When dealing with mortals, she will often take the form of a dwarven master mason, architect or planner. She gives excellent advice, and is secretly the mind behind many of the architectural marvels of the world, thought most of them have been lost to time.
